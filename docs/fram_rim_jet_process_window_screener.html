What changed: the narrow-rim mode uses a small mechanical contact patch for pressure/torque while using a separate thermal influence area for heat capacity and conduction. This avoids the old square-cube artifact where a 1 mm wheel width made the action-zone thermal mass collapse unrealistically.

Calibration note: the “Load exported calibration” button reproduces the exported annular-style calibration as closely as possible from the PDF/CSV settings. The “Load 50×1 rim + jet” button converts to a 50 mm diameter × 1 mm wide rim with a 6 mm / 80 m·s⁻¹ perpendicular side jet.
